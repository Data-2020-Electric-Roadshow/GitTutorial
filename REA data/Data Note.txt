1. Annual Stats 1941:
This data is from REA 1941 Annual Statistical Report, and has not merged with information like borrower's geographical information or their name.

2. 1941 map data:
Based on "Annual Stats 1941", but add borrower's information including their zipcode, latitude, longitude, name, city, and served county. This information
is extracted from "REA Borrower List.pdf". However, please be aware that, during merging process, only about 85.6% (747/872) of borrowers of 
"Annual Stats 1941" are successfully matched.
